Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

Community Transports LLC collects personal information necessary to evaluate driver applications, including your name, email address, and responses to eligibility questions. We also collect information about the school districts you can service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and evaluate your driver application
  • Communicate with you about your application status
  • Coordinate required background checks and drug testing
  • Maintain records in compliance with Texas transportation regulations

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Background check and drug testing service providers
  • School districts where you may provide services
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit and at rest, secure password hashing, and role-based access controls.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

6. Data Retention

We retain your application information for a period of 3 years from the date of submission, or as required by Texas law, whichever is longer.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 21. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 21 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:
